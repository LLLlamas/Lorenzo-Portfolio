Questions

Good to know

Do I need an account to use the app?

No. Llamas Cookbook works fully offline the moment you install it. An account is only needed if you want optional iCloud sync across devices.

How does photo import work?

Take a photo of any recipe — a cookbook page, a screenshot, a handwritten card — and the app's AI extracts the ingredients and steps into a clean, editable recipe. You can review and adjust anything before you cook.

Which devices are supported?

iPhone on iOS 18 and later. iPad support is on the roadmap.

Can I cancel the subscription anytime?

Yes. Pro is billed through your Apple account and you can cancel anytime in App Store settings. Your recipes stay on your device either way.

Is my data private?

Your recipes are stored on your device. Photos you import are processed only to extract the recipe and are not used for anything else.
